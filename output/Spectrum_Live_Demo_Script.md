# Spectrum Live Demo Script

## Slide 1
Spectrum is Google Analytics for the human side of AI conversations.
Today, transcripts tell us what was said, but not how the human felt, where they hesitated, or whether they moved toward a decision.

## Slide 2
That creates a real problem for teams reviewing AI calls.
Long calls are slow to review, emotion and behavior get lost, and teams still do not know what to do next after the call.

## Slide 3
If you only look at the transcript, you can read the sentence, but you miss the human signal.
You do not see the pause before the answer, the confidence rise, or whether the AI helped calm the conversation or made it worse.

## Slide 4
Spectrum solves that with a single-call voice analytics workbench.
It is built to help analysts do four jobs in one place: investigate what happened, score the human response, coach from real signals, and decide the next action.

## Slide 5
This is the core demo moment.
The waveform becomes an analytics surface.
We show the full 30-minute call, emotion clusters directly on the waveform, a heatband underneath for fast scanning, a background-noise track, and emoji-based raw emotion highlights for key moments.

## Slide 6
That means one call supports the full workflow.
You can investigate key moments, score confidence and hesitation, coach where the human struggled, and decide whether to follow up, escalate, qualify, or flag risk.

## Slide 7
Spectrum turns AI conversations into something teams can act on.
Sales teams can spot readiness, support teams can catch frustration, and AI teams can measure whether the agent builds trust or creates friction.
